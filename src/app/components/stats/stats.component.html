Basic parameters

{{selectedHero.attack}}

The attack affects the damage dealt by this hero.

{{selectedHero.defence}}

Defense affects the amount of damage absorbed.

{{selectedHero.health}}

Health affects the survivability of this hero in battle.

{{selectedHero.rage}}

Rage affects the ability to impose a penalty on the enemy.

{{selectedHero.willpower}}

Willpower affects the ability to resist penalties.

{{selectedHero.dmgReducedBy * 100}}%

Absorption affects the amount of pure damage absorbed (stacks with defense).

{{selectedHero.canCross}}

Mobility affects the number of cells a hero can move in a turn.
