Welcome to the North.

I see the storm hasn't spared you, but you've made it—and that's more than many can say. There are no easy days here. Frost, wind, and darkness—these are your new companions.

From this moment on, you are part of the Watch. Not heroes, not prisoners, and not soldiers. Simply those who stand on the Wall so that others can sleep peacefully. Rest, warm up, and get your gear from the quartermaster. Training begins tomorrow. In the meantime… look at the Wall. It's higher than it looks from the shore.

As a brother of the Night's Watch, you will be able to defend the kingdoms from external threats. Here, you will also receive forgiveness for all your sins, and most importantly, this is a wonderful opportunity for an adventure that will last a lifetime.
