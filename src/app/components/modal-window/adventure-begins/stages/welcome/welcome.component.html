Welcome to the Watch

The North calls. The thin line on the Wall is held by those who swore an oath—and now you are among them.

Why are you here?

In the north, there is eternal darkness and cold winds. The Night's Watch is the shield that protects the realms of men. You join the ranks of those who stand on the Wall, patrol abandoned fortresses, and love order more than warm hearths.

Your first step

Immediately after this window, you'll choose your first hero—the leader of a small squad. Each has their own specialization, skill tree, and starting equipment.

It's time to say the oath

"Night gathers, and my watch begins. It will not end until I die. I will take no wife, no lands, no father to children. I will wear no crown, nor seek glory. I will live and die at my post. I am a sword in the darkness, I am a watchman on the Wall, I am the fire that drives away the cold, I am the light that brings dawn, I am the horn that wakes the sleeping, I am the shield that guards the realm of men. I pledge my life and honor to the Night's Watch this night, and all that will come."
